Course Descriptions

Start Here

Intro to Design and Innovation

BUSI 220 · 3 cr · Hesam Panahi

You'll learn to empathize with people unlike yourself, synthesize messy qualitative data into insights, and prototype solutions through hands-on fieldwork and design challenges. This is where you discover that anyone can be a designer.

"Don't be afraid to throw away what you built."

New Enterprises: Discovery (Disco)

BUSI 221 · 3 cr · Hesam Panahi

You'll explore industries that fascinate you, conduct informational interviews with real stakeholders, and map stepping stones from where you are to where you want to go. You'll leave with a portfolio of opportunities and a clearer sense of your path.

"There are more paths available to me than I thought."

The Founder's Journey

BUSI 360 · 1 cr

You'll hear directly from founders about the real, messy, human side of building companies — then reflect on what surprised you and what it means for your own entrepreneurial potential.

"If people can see it, they believe they can be it."

Go Deeper

New Enterprises

BUSI 369 · 3 cr

You'll take a real problem and build a venture concept around it — conducting primary market research, testing your value proposition with real customers, and learning to systematically eliminate risk before you build.

"The way to start a business is by having early contact with customers and users."

Design Build Ship (DBS)

BUSI 365 · 3 cr · Hesam Panahi

You'll work in sprints to build and test prototypes with real users, analyze what you learn, and decide whether to persevere, pivot, or stop. You'll ship something real — not just a class project.

"Shipping something imperfect teaches you more than planning something perfect."

New Venture Competition (NVC)

BUSI 469 · 3 cr

You'll run your own startup with structured coaching — setting milestones, running go-to-market experiments, and making tangible market progress. This is where ideas become real companies.

"You can start a business while in college."

Specialize

Tech Product Design & Development (TPDD)

BUSI 222 · 3 cr

You'll learn to think like a product manager — conducting customer interviews, building empathy maps, writing PRDs, and shipping prototypes at the intersection of technology, business, and design.

"Good products come from understanding people, not just technology."

Business Model Economics (BME)

BUSI 223 · 3 cr

You'll build financial models for startups from scratch — identifying assumptions, tracking them against reality, and iterating with increasing resolution. No finance background needed.

"A financial model is a prototype — it requires being generative, creative, and iterative."

Entrepreneurial Communications

BUSI 361 · 3 cr

You'll practice presenting in business contexts, networking professionally, writing effective emails, running meetings, and managing conflicts — the everyday skills that make everything else work.

"Communication is a skill you can practice and improve, not a talent you're born with."

Financing New Ventures

BUSI 461 · 3 cr

You'll build TAM/SAM/SOM models, evaluate term-sheet economics, craft pitch decks, and develop fundraising strategies. You'll learn that how you finance your business shapes what it can become.

"There are many different ways to finance many different types of businesses."

Entrepreneurial Strategy

BUSI 463 · 3 cr

You'll analyze case studies, navigate founder dilemmas with no clear right answer, and develop integrated strategy recommendations. Every strategic choice opens some doors and closes others.

"Strategy is about choosing what NOT to do, not just what to do."

Student Venture Fund (SVF)

BUSI 465 · 3 cr

You'll evaluate real ventures from the investor perspective — constructing investment theses, performing due diligence, and delivering formal recommendations to an Investment Committee.

"The skills of evaluating ventures are the same skills you need to build better ones."
